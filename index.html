About us

The purpose of this website is to provide easier access to all different types of information without having to surf through the internet. In the present world, knowledge is lost in many different ways. Whether it's the actions of our ancestors or our own current actions, a lot of it is being lost. We hope to save as much knowledge as possible by storing it all in one place where it is easy to access it. It is a hope to make a database that is easy to navigate.
